Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

Summary: We collect only the social media data needed to display your analytics. Your access tokens are stored securely in our database. We do not sell, share, or retain your data on our servers beyond what is needed to keep you connected.

MoverMetrics ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our social media analytics application.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account, we request the following data via TikTok's official API:

  • Profile information: Your TikTok display name, username, and profile picture URL.
  • Account statistics: Your public follower count, following count, and total likes received.
  • Video data: A list of your posted videos including titles, cover image URLs, view counts, like counts, comment counts, and publish timestamps.
  • Access token: A temporary OAuth access token used to retrieve the above data from TikTok's API.

We do not collect your TikTok password, email address, private messages, or any data beyond the scopes listed above.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Display your TikTok analytics within the application (follower counts, video performance, engagement metrics, growth trends).
  • Calculate derived statistics such as average views, engagement rates, and monthly performance summaries.
  • Build a local follower history to show growth sparklines across sessions.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond what is described above.

3. Data Storage

Your access tokens are stored securely in our database (Supabase) associated with your user account. This allows you to remain connected across sessions without needing to re-authorize. This includes:

  • Your TikTok access token (stored in Supabase, tied to your authenticated user account).
  • Your follower count history (used to generate sparklines, stored locally in your browser).

Analytics data (video stats, follower counts) is fetched fresh from the platform APIs each session and is not persistently stored. When you click "Disconnect," your token is removed from our database and all locally cached data is cleared.

4. Token Exchange Proxy

When you complete TikTok OAuth authorization, TikTok issues a temporary authorization code. To exchange this code for an access token, we use a secure server-side proxy hosted on Supabase. This proxy:

  • Receives the authorization code and your redirect URI.
  • Exchanges them with TikTok's API for an access token.
  • Returns the access token directly to your browser.
  • The access token is securely stored in our Supabase database, tied to your user account, so you remain connected across sessions.
  • Does not log or retain your temporary authorization code beyond the exchange process.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information or TikTok data with any third parties, except:

  • TikTok: Data is retrieved directly from TikTok's API on your behalf.
  • Supabase: Used as a secure token exchange proxy and for storing your access tokens tied to your user account.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal process.

6. Data Retention

Access tokens are retained in our Supabase database for as long as your account is active. Local follower history data is stored in your browser. You control both:

  • Click "Disconnect" in the application (removes your token from Supabase and clears all locally cached TikTok data).
  • Clear your browser's localStorage or cookies (removes locally cached data; token in Supabase remains until you disconnect).
  • Revoke access in your TikTok account settings (invalidates the token; you should also disconnect within the app to clean up stored data).

7. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics scripts (such as Google Analytics), or any third-party tracking technologies on this application. The only browser storage used is localStorage for the purposes described in this policy.

8. Children's Privacy

This Service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

9. Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your data, including using HTTPS for all communications, a server-side proxy to avoid exposing API secrets client-side, and not storing any sensitive data on our servers. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

10. Your Rights and Controls

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All data is displayed directly in the application.
  • Deletion: Click "Disconnect" to immediately delete all stored data.
  • Revocation: Revoke access anytime in your TikTok app settings under "Apps & Websites."

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
